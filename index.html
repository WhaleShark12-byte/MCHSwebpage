MCHS Portrait of a Learner

What Every
Raider
Becomes.

McCreary Central graduates are equipped with the knowledge, skills, and character to thrive in college, career, and community. Our Portrait of a Learner defines what that looks like.

Critical Thinker

Raiders analyze, evaluate, and create — approaching problems with curiosity and evidence-based reasoning.

Effective Communicator

Articulating ideas clearly and respectfully in writing, speaking, and digital formats across all audiences.

Responsible Citizen

Engaged in community, ethical in decision-making, and aware of their impact on the world around them.

Creative Problem Solver

Embracing innovation, persistence, and collaboration to meet real-world challenges with confidence.

Career & College Ready

Armed with credentials, certifications, and experience to succeed in the next chapter — whatever that looks like.

Culturally Aware

Respecting diversity, including military-connected students, and building a welcoming, inclusive school community.

Collaborative Leader

Working with others, lifting teammates, and stepping up to lead when the team needs it most.

Self-Directed Learner

Setting goals, managing time, and taking ownership of their academic journey from day one.
